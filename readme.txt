~~~ PANCAKE GWRAWRURULRG - V 1.0.0 ~~~

Pancake GWRAWRURULRG is a pancake themed clicker game in Ukagaka form. Stack your pancakes as high as they'll go while collecting a variety of tools and charms to help you along the way!

Even when the stack is high, and the pancakes are sizzling away, maybe there might even be new wonders in your next pancake life...
